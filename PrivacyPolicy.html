Privacy Policy

Your privacy and the security of your personal health information are our top priorities. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number, email)
  • Medical and dental history
  • Insurance information
  • Treatment records and clinical notes
  • Appointment scheduling information
  • Payment and billing information

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, and usage patterns.

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our dental services
  • Process appointments and manage your treatment
  • Communicate with you about your care
  • Process payments and insurance claims
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Send you important updates about our practice

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare providers involved in your treatment
  • Insurance companies for claims processing
  • Laboratory services for dental work
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business associates who assist in our operations

We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Staff training on privacy and security protocols
  • Physical safeguards for paper records

Under HIPAA and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your medical records
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Request restrictions on how we use your information
  • Receive a copy of this privacy notice
  • File a complaint about our privacy practices
  • Request alternative communication methods
